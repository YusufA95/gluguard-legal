GluGuard — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026
Developer: Yubay Labs · Contact: yubaylays@gmail.com

GluGuard (“the app”) is a mobile app where people with celiac disease and gluten sensitivity find and share safe places to eat. This policy explains what data we process, why, and with whom.

Data we collect

  • Account information: email address, username, display name, dietary type (celiac / gluten sensitivity / other), an optional bio and profile photo. Passwords are stored encrypted (hashed) by our authentication provider.
  • Location: your device location, used to show nearby places on the map. You can decline this permission and still use the app by searching for a city. Your location is not continuously tracked.
  • Camera and photos: photos you take to scan a product label/barcode. The label photo is sent to an AI provider (Google Gemini) to analyze its contents. If you are signed in, your scan results are saved to your history.
  • User content: the reviews you write, safety/food ratings, cross-contamination notes, photos you upload, and venue lists you save.
  • Notification token: if you enable notifications, a device token used to send push notifications.

We do not collect data for advertising, and we do not sell your data.

Who we share data with (service providers)

  • Supabase — database, authentication, file storage (EU/Ireland).
  • Google (Gemini API) — reading label photos with AI.
  • Open Food Facts — product-content lookup by barcode.
  • OpenStreetMap / Photon / Overpass / OpenFreeMap — map, search, and venue discovery.
  • RevenueCat & Google Play Billing — (when a subscription is enabled) purchase processing and subscription management; payment and card details are handled only by Google Play and are never stored by us.
  • Expo — push notification delivery.

Retention and deletion

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data (reviews, photos, saved lists, scan history, and your dietary info) in two ways:

  • In the app: Profile tab → Delete account. The action cannot be undone and starts immediately.
  • Via web / email: the deletion instructions page or yubaylays@gmail.com.

Deletion covers your photos in storage and your database records; we complete the request within 30 days at the latest. Records we are legally required to keep (e.g. payment/invoice records) may be retained for the applicable legal period.

Children’s privacy

The app is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect data from them (the applicable digital-consent age in the EU; this may range between 13–16 in some countries).

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time; we will announce significant changes in the app or on this page.
