GluGuard — Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: 18 June 2026 · Contact: yubaylays@gmail.com

You can permanently delete your GluGuard account and all data associated with it at any time. There are two ways to do this.

1. From within the app (instant)

  1. Open GluGuard and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to the Profile tab at the bottom.
  3. Tap the red “Delete account” link at the bottom of the page.
  4. Confirm “Delete everything” in the dialog that appears.

This action cannot be undone.

2. By email

If you can’t access the app, simply email an “account deletion request” to yubaylays@gmail.com from the email address you used to create your account. We will complete the request within 30 days at the latest.

Data that is deleted

  • Your account and profile information (email, username, dietary info, bio).
  • Your reviews, ratings, cross-contamination notes, and all photos you uploaded.
  • Your saved venue lists, scan history, and notification token.
  • Your customer record with the payment provider (Stripe), if any.

Data that may be retained

Records we are legally required to keep (e.g. payment and invoice records for tax/accounting purposes) may be retained for the applicable legal period. Apart from these, your data is deleted in full.
